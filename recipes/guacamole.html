Dish Details

Guacamole is DELICIOUS and unbelievably easy to make! With just a handful of ingredients

and a few steps you will have a wholesome simple taste of Mexico in your own home

This one is a keeper for the regular-healthy-meal rotation

Guacamole is a creamy, avocado-based dip that's most commonly associated with Mexican food.

It's typically eaten with chips or as a topping on tacos or various other delicious meals.

Dish Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps to Prepare Dish

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.

  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  3. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
